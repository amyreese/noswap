I am a senior engineer, writing Python professionally since 2010, and working
on foundational infrastructure and tooling to support millions of engineers,
data scientists, and ML researchers. I am also the maintainer of numerous
open source libraries on PyPI, and an experienced speaker from Python
conferences and meetups around the world.

I wrote the static site generator [Nib][] that powers this site, as well
the static link tree generator [Plinky][] to manage my [contact page][contact].
